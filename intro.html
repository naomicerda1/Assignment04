Kali Uchis is a singer, songwriter, producer, as well as a video director. In 2012 Kali Uchis, otherwise known as Karli-Marina Loaiza, self-released her mixtape and within three years released one major album but made quite a few singles with major collaborations with famous artists. She is one of my favorite because she really puts her heart and soul into her work.
