Who Is Kali Uchis?

Kali Uchis was born July 17, 1993 in Pereira, Colombia but was raised in Alexandria, Virginia. Her parents are native Colombians, which is where she spends most of her down time giving back to the less fortunate.

In July of 2018, she went back to Pereira, Colombia to donate medicine, food, school supplies, shoes, and household appliances. She even made modifications to homes for families suffering from illnesses or those who had nothing.

Growing up Kali Uchis loved poetry. She later joined a jazz band and played the saxophone in high school.
